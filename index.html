Projects

The Shape of Movies - Computer Vision & Web development

A website about visualizing the colors of movies.

I developed the offline program to get the color palette of every frame of a movie, with OpenCV. Then I made this website to have an interactive viz of my results, with MeteorJS, ReactJS and PixiJS.

Believe it or not, but the design inspiration for the website comes from me looking at maths papers online, all made with LaTeX. I sure hate LaTeX, but I really like the plainness it gives. I wanted to apply the same philosophy to movie analysis. Other cool projects like the Colors of Motion or Cinemetrics inspired my focus on movie color palettes.
