Ubisoft Paris built collab teams supporting Massive Entertainment on *Star Wars Outlaws* and Ubisoft Singapore on *Skull & Bones*, two AAA open-world titles. It also launched a game production on Unreal Engine 5 as lead studio.

- Senior designer for an undisclosed strategy game
- Feature owner and designer for Kessel Sabacc and Fathier Races minigames in *Star Wars Outlaws*, leading implementation in the Snowdrop engine using visual scripting
- Speaker at GDC 2025 for 2 talks: *Crafting Sabacc in 'Star Wars Outlaws'* and *Designing Emotions in 'Star Wars Outlaws' Sabacc NPCs*
- Designer on The Helm, an end-game PvPvE feature in *Skull & Bones*, leading its implementation in the Anvil pipeline
- Developed tooling scripts and web platforms to improve team workflows (Python, Meteor, React, GraphQL)
